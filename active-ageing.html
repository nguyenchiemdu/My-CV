Active Ageing Vietnam

Organized by HEC Montréal, in Canada, Social Business Creation (SBC) is a global competition and an innovative training platform through which participants learn about how to use market mechanisms to solve social challenges.

Developed as part of the Social Business Creation, Active Ageing Vietnam assists planning for retirement to ensure financial security and health care access.

With the Active Ageing Vietnam project, we won the international award “Impactful Social Business Idea”.
